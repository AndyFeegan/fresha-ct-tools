⚠

The Leap Trap

You hear a keyword and jump straight to the Fresha feature. You never find out how big the problem actually is.

Bad: Prospect says "we have no-shows." You immediately pitch Fresha reminders. Turns out it's 3% of bookings — not worth solving.

Good: Prospect says "we have no-shows." You ask: "Walk me through last week's no-shows — how many out of how many bookings?" Now you know the real size before you pitch.
