Feature Tour vs P.A.I.N. Demo

Feature tour: "Here's the calendar. You can drag and drop. Colour-code by stylist. Filter by service type." The prospect thinks: "Cool. But does this solve my problem?"

P.A.I.N. demo: "You said your biggest pain is capturing bookings when the phone is ringing. Look at this booking page — clients see real-time availability and book instantly. No phone call. No voicemail." The prospect thinks: "That's me in there."

The Mid-Demo Pivot

Signals: short answers, no questions, "hm" instead of "oh interesting." If the prospect isn't leaning in, your discovery was thin.

The fix: pause the demo, go back to discovery. "Sarah, I want to make sure I'm showing you the right stuff. When you think about the booking problem, is the pain more about missing calls or about rescheduling cancellations?" Listen. Then come back to the demo. A mid-demo pivot isn't a failure — it's a correction.
